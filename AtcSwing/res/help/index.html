ATC - Air Traffic Control

Version: 1.1.0

GOAL.

Your goal is to maneuver safely the planes in your area.

You need to:

  • take off planes waiting at runways;
  • land the planes at destination runways;
  • maneuver the planes out of destination points at altitude of 36000 feet.

The simulation ends when:

  • user requests to exit the simulation;
  • a plane collision is detected, the collision happens when the distance between two planes are lower then 4 nautical miles and the altitude difference is lower then 1000 feet;
  • a wrong destination is reached;
  • a plane crash is reported whenever a plane lands in wrong direction.

Scores are sorted in order of the number of planes safe. Suspending a game is not permitted.
